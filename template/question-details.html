What is the Harry Potter Kano Coding Kit?

The Harry Potter Kano Coding Kit is a DIY kit that lets you perform spell motions inspired by the Wizarding World of Harry Potter. It includes over 40+ challenges that let you make serpents slither, toads transfigure, fires flow, feathers float, goblets duplicate, Bertie Bott's Every Flavour Beans enlarge, and more.

How does the Harry Potter Kano Coding Kit work?

Your Coding Wand will fall asleep when not in use and can be picked up and woken up immediately, by pressing the button on the top.

In this deep sleep, it can last at least two weeks.
